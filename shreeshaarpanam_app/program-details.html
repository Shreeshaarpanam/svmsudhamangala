Program Details:

The present incumbent to this hallowed Pīṭha, paramapūjya Sri Vidyashreesha Theertharu a scholar-par-excellence and unequivocally revered by one and all, is continuing to carry forward the tradition and in many ways has restored them to newer heights. He provides his scholarly expositions to seekers on the crown jewel of Dvaita tattvaśāstra, śrī ṭīkakr̥tpādas magnum opus, śrīman nyāyasudhā, alongwith the three jewels that adorn the crown further beautifying it, the three works of Śrī vyāsarājaru - Tattva Chandrikā, Nyāyāmruta, Tarkatāṇḍava, collectively called "Vyāsatrāya" along with the academic pursuits he has been actively pursuing the all-round development of the Śrīmaṭha, since his ascension. The seed in the form of Sri Vyāsatīrtha vidhyāpeeṭha sown 10 years ago by him in his Pūrvāśrama watered and nourished after his ordainment has grown rapidly to become a tree that, flowered and is bearing the first set of fruits this year.

The vidhyāpeeṭha is extremely proud to announce the graduation of its highly talented first batch of Srīman Nyāyasudhā Scholars in Mysore. The four students who have studied śrīman Nyāyasudhā along with the Vyāsatrāya under the guidance of paramapūjya Sri Vidyashreesha Theertharu ,will be formally tested by eminent scholars and Pīṭhādhipatigālu during the Śrīmān Nyāyasudhā maṅgaḷa mahōtsava.

Disciples and devotees of the Śrīmaṭha have also resolved to perform the sahasra chandra darśana śānti of Paramapūjya śrīpādaru, which is coincinding with the Sudhā maṅgaḷa mahōtsava Both the events will be held under the fitting appellations Śrīśārpaṇam and viśva pāvana vaibhavam, from 7th through 9th of June. Several grand programs have also been planned to be conducted on these days. As a preamble, the students would be giving examinations in the holy presence of several Saints from the 2nd of June. In addition, the following programs would be undertaken during this period:

  • A grand procession of Vyāsatraya and SrimanNyayaSudha granthagaḷu
  • Special Durbar on the newly constructed silver throne bedecked with diamonds.
  • Tantrasarokta Homas (72 Mahamantra) for prosperity of the world
  • Vēda and sarvamūla pārāyaṇa
  • Variety of Cultural programs by eminent artistes.

There is no doubt that these programs, held in the divine presence of several Pīṭhādhipatigaḷu, will provide an excellent opportunity for the spiritual seekers to engross themselves in a festive fervor. We earnestly desire and request that everyone participate in these programs wholeheartedly in whichever way possible and obtain the blessings of Sri Hari Vayu Gurugalu without fail.
